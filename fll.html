FIRST® Lego® League

The FIRST® Lego® League (FLL) is a robotics program designed for students in grades K-8. It focuses on engaging young minds in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM) through hands-on learning experiences. FLL teams design, build, and program robots using LEGO Mindstorms kits to complete various challenges based on a yearly theme. The program emphasizes teamwork, problem-solving, and innovation. Here are some key aspects of the FLL program:

This page was written in compliance with FLL Branding Guidelines

Aspect Description
Team Structure FLL teams typically consist of 4-10 students, along with adult coaches and mentors who guide them through the season.
Robot Design Teams design and build robots using LEGO Mindstorms kits, focusing on creativity and engineering principles.
Competition FLL competitions involve completing missions on a themed playing field, as well as presenting research projects related to the annual theme.
Core Values FLL promotes core values such as Gracious Professionalism® and Coopertition®, encouraging respect and collaboration among teams.
